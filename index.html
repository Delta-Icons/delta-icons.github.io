Delta

Now on a platform near you

Delta is a passion project by me, Leif Niemczik, open sourced and helped by awesome people. Starting off as a beautification for my homescreen it has since become a top-ranking app in Google Play Store and was featured in the #myandroid-campaign as well as quite a few places on the internet. With over a million downloads and 2700+ icons I want to open it up to other platforms like iOS and macOS with the help of the community that built around it.

So if you feel like helping just head over to GitHub and see if you find something you'd like to add, or just enjoy your new homescreen ☺️

Special thanks to Dani Mahardhika for his awesome Candybar Dashboard that is powering the Android Version of Android.
