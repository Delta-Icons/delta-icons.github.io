Delta

Now on a platform near you

Delta is a passion project by me, Leif Niemczik, open sourced and helped by awesome people. Starting off as a beautification for my homescreen it has since become a top-ranking app in Google Play and was featured in the #myandroid-campaign as well as quite a few places on the internet. With over a million downloads and icons. I want to open it up to other platforms like iOS and macOS with the help of the community that built around it.
